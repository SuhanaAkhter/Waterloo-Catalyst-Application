The Catalyst Program and I

Why do I Want to Join?

The Catalyst program would be an amazing opportunity, and an engaging and fun, but also educational way to use a part of my summer. I would most look forward to meeting and working with many like-minded people. The collaborative aspects of this program seem like they would lead to various interesting conversations and ideas and would also further improve my collaboration skills.

Attending talks and workshops from Waterloo graduates and faculty is a very unique aspect of this program and I think that I would enjoy and learn a lot from it. This program would also be a great way to get a feel for university life, particularly because it focuses on STEM and leadership, which are fields that I’d like to look deeper into. I plan to apply to an engineering program at the University of Waterloo and I think that this summer program would be a great chance to further explore the different aspects of engineering and get to know the university better.

Why should you accept me?

I think that I would be a great addition to this program for several reasons. First of all, as I mentioned in my about me video, I have participated in various STEM-related programs and have many technical skills, but I am also very keen to learn more. Creativity has always been one of my strengths, and I’ve used it for problem-solving as well as in projects. For example, I would often seek reading recommendations, but I found that most websites reviewed the same well-known books, so I channeled my creative energy and made a website featuring reviews of lesser-known books.

Apart from creativity, I’ve also developed effective collaboration and leadership skills from working with various groups of people at camps and events. The Catalyst program would be the ideal opportunity for me this summer, as I am very open-minded and eager to learn, so I know that I would get lots out of the experience.
